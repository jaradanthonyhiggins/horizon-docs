# Donate to Horizon

<Banner variant="success">
Your support keeps Horizon running and evolving!  
Even a small contribution helps fund server costs, development, and new features.
</Banner>

---

## Quick Donation Steps

<Steps>
1. Click the **Donate via PayPal** button below.
2. Enter the amount you wish to contribute.
3. Complete the payment using your PayPal account or credit/debit card.
</Steps>

<Warning>
Don’t forget to **leave your Discord username** with your donation.  
Without it, we won’t be able to link your account or give you your benefits.
</Warning>
<Danger>
Please do not attempt to request a refund or reverse your donation. Doing so will result in a permanent blacklist from Horizon.
</Danger>
